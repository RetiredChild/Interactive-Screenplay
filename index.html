INT. RAILWAY STATION - DAY

A CHONKY RED BAG goes through the scanner at the station entry and it comes out on the other side. A girl’s hand lifts the bag, with a lot of effort and keeps it on the ground. We see two pair of foot, one girl, one boy, the girl tries to lift the heavy bag by it’s straps.

VARUN (O.S.)

Njan edukatte?

NITHYA (O.S.)

(struggling)
Illa, kuzhappamila... I can... manage.

The bag has two strap handles, Nithya’s hand is holding both the straps tightly, Varun’s hand grabs onto one.

VARUN

It’s okay, platform 3 vare pokande.

Nithya doesn’t let go off the strap.

VARUN (CONT’D)

Let it go, Nithya.

Her fingers hesitate and she lets go of one.

EACH GRABBING ONE STRAP, they walk to the escalator in sync as the bag swings between them.

Standing close on the escalator, THEIR HANDS RUB AGAINST EACH OTHER slightly, and knowingly both of them push their hands against each other while holding onto the bag.

As the escalator comes up, we see both of them. VARUN (24), lean, dark and shabbily dressed. NITHYA (24), in a long, loose kurti. She wears thick prescribed glasses. They reach the top and walk slowly on the bridge. Varun is looking down and Nithya has been looking at him, trying to catch his sight.

Varun takes the step down to the platform and Nithya holds back the bag and she stands still.

VARUN (CONT’D)

Train ippo varum.

NITHYA

(Suresh Gopi tone)
Ingott vaa Varune...

Varun walks up. He has a confused look on his face, he sees Nithya’s eyes glimmer.

VARUN

Entha? Last minute odi keyaran aano?

NITHYA

Athalla! Nee angott nokku.

Varun looks where Nithya is pointing. There is a BRIGHT ORANGE SUNSET where the train tracks disappear.

NITHYA (CONT’D)

Ithokke kaanathe train okke pidichitt enth cheyyana...

Both hands release the handle. The bag falls and... THEIR FINGERS HUG tightly.

VARUN

Train pidichitt enthina...

There is silence... the sun slowly goes down, people rush across the bridge to catch the train.

NITHYA

You should go, platform ticket polum edukathe, pokkiyal 200 roopa fine kodukkendi varum.

VARUN

Train keyatti vittittenkilum pokan sammadikkedai.

NITHYA

It’s a free country man, you don’t need anyone’s sammadham to be here.

VARUN

Irangy pode ennum parayum, pokendennum parayum... psychosisnte pala avasthakalum keettittund...

NITHYA

Pala avasthanthrangalum...

Varun smiles... suddenly the lady on the speaker comes in—

ANNOUNCEMENT

Yathrakar daivayi shredhikuka, Train number 12625...

ANNOUNCEMENT (CONT’D)

Thiruvananthapurath ninnum New Delhi’kk pokunna... Kerala express,

They start hurrying down the stairs.

ANNOUNCEMENT (CONT’D)

Nishchitha samayathil ninnum 20 minute vyki odunnu... ningalkundaya asoukaryathil...

They stop rushing... they stop.

VARUN

Enna pinne pathuke nadanna pore.

They walk through the platform slowly...

NITHYA

Thirich poyitt entha parupadi?

VARUN

Aahhh... Musthafade koode vykitt irangum...

NITHYA

Innale njan medichu kodutha shawarmade cash avan thannilla...

VARUN

Ath charitykk koduthathann vicharicho...

NITHYA

Ivan okke engane oru CA aakunne.

VARUN

Avane konde athokke pattu...

NITHYA

Mmmm... Nee thirich bus book cheytho?

VARUN

Illa nee poyitt nokkam enn vechu.

NITHYA

(To the shopkeeper)
Oru cheriya packet Goodday... rand chaya.

She pays the cash, takes the tea. Varun takes a sip and his face changes.

VARUN

Vendayirunn...

NITHYA

Monu Coimbatore paayasam polathe chaaya alle kittunne... ath vech nokkumbo ithokke nallatha.

VARUN

Pinne Delhi’l kiddilam chaya alle.

NITHYA

Kiddilam aanu, sabardasth. Vannal alle ariyu... South Indian border kadakatha tum jaise dardravasikko mei kya bolun.

VARUN

Ee semester break nee avide ninnirunnel njan vannene...

NITHYA

Aahha, poya ann thott amma thirich vilikunnathalle, naattil vannillayirunnel, amma train pidich vann enne alakkiyene.

VARUN

Next sem break’n njan angott varam, Coimbatore to Delhi... 1 day 17 hours...

NITHYA

Pinne...

VARUN

Nee nokkiko...

NITHYA

Mmm... Vannal Njan nokkikolam...

Varun hides his smile with the tea cup, he waits while Nithya takes the last sip. She puts HER CUP IN HIS and throws it into the dustbin.

They sit on a bench, Nithya is thinking something and searching her pocket, Varun checks the watch...

NITHYA

Njan earphones edukkan marannoo... ennoru doubt illathilla.

She opens her bag and checks.

VARUN

Enthelumokke marakkanam... athanu oru keezhvazhakkam...

NITHYA

Ninak ath sthiram parupadi ayond kuzhappamilla... 2 divasam train’l irunn njan pazhukkum.

Varun takes HIS EARPHONE out and offers.

NITHYA

Avalu innale rathri eduthatha... ravile thirich medikkan marannu njan.

Varun stills holding out his in front of her.

NITHYA

Kollam... nalla earphone...

VARUN

Inna vecho ennu...

NITHYA

Nah... kuzhappamilladai.

VARUN

Just keep it Nithya. 2 divasam ille, cinemayum download cheyth vechathalle.... Pinne... vidhoorathayil nokki patt kett irikathe engane nee travel cheyum?

NITHYA

Njan book vayicholam.

She takes out and shows a book.

VARUN

10 page vayich thirich veykum... What if... chumma dialogue adikkan arelum vann aduth irunnal, how would you avoid.

NITHYA

Eda it’s okay, you need it no.

VARUN

I’ll buy one later...

NITHYA

I’ll also buy one later.

VARUN

(Tone starts to change)
But dai, ninakk ippozhathe avishyam alle.

NITHYA

(Irritated)
Bro, I am saying it’s fine.

Varun puts back the earphone. They both sit silently.

MUSIC SLOWLY RISES IN THE BACKGROUND

Nithya opens her mouth to say something... twice but she has nothing to say and she looks away.

The clock keeps running. Nithya gets a call from her mom. She attends the call.

They hear a train siren in the distance. In sync they both look to the direction—

THE TRAIN SLOWLY PULLS INTO THE STATION.

Nithya takes the bag, all by herself, before Varun can reach for it. She gets in, Varun waits outside. Nithya finds her seat and keeps her bag there and rushes out.

They both stand near the door, Varun with one leg inside.

NITHYA

Enna nee poyko.

VARUN

Bro... it’s fine.

Nithya starts tearing up a bit. The horn blares and Varun gets out. The train starts to move and Varun walks with the pace.

NITHYA

Enthedai Mani Ratnam cinema scenea...

VARUN

Athile heroine kurachoode easy aanu.

NITHYA

Enthinu easy?

Suddenly a man, runs in, trying to get in, Nithya has to move away and Varun has to stop walking and by this time the train has caught pace.

Nithya looks outside and waves at him. Varun waves back.

NITHYA

Appo nee May’l varoole?

VARUN

Nokkaam...

NITHYA

Nalla choodayirikkum...

VARUN

(Yelling)
Sahicholam...

The train moves away from the station. Varun walks up the stairs. He sees the train disappear... where the sun had.

INT. TRAIN - SAME TIME

Nithya is looking out, at her town, that’s moving past her. Her eyes start watering. Her vision moves inside and an aunty sitting opposite to her has been staring at her.

RANDOM AUNTY

Cheettan aano?

NITHYA

Aaru?

RANDOM AUNTY

Koode, keyatti vidan vannath?

NITHYA

Alla friend.

RANDOM AUNTY

Ohh... Molu... Delhilottano atho..

NITHYA

Delhi...

RANDOM AUNTY

Avide padikkuano?

NITHYA

Mmm...

Nithya takes her phone and then puts her hand inside her pocket and remembers...

RANDOM AUNTY

Ippo nalla thanuppanalle avide... Enikk avide poyitt venam sweater okke medikkan...

Nithya just nods and smiles. She then takes the book and pretends to read it.

RANDOM AUNTY (CONT’D)

Delhi’l evideya mol nikkunne? Lajpath Nagar ariyuo?

Nithya’s face reads hopeless... and if her hands weren’t occupied, she would have face palmed.

INT. RAILWAY STATION - SAME TIME

Varun is walking out of the station. He is on a call and gets stopped by railway police and a TT.

POLICE

Ticket.

VARUN

(On the call)
Eda oru pani kitty, njan ippo vilikkam.

The police looks at him up and down. He cuts the call.

POLICE

Drop cheyyan vannathano? Platform ticket.

VARUN

Eduthilla...

POLICE

Athenthe edukathe.

VARUN

Marannu poyi, pettann rush cheyth, train pidikkan...
